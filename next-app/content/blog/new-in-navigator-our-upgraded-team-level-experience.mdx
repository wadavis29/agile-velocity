This summer we've been focusing on enhancing the team-level experience in Path to Agility Navigator. Our newest features include an Agile Outcome side panel, new capability and prioritization views, Team Dashboard, and streamlined progress visualization.

The result: Path to Agility Navigator makes it easier than ever to manage, track, and show progress on your Teams' Agile capability development and prioritize the capabilities you should focus on next.

In this video, Path to Agility CEO David Hawks walks through the latest new features in Path to Agility Navigator and our improved workflow.

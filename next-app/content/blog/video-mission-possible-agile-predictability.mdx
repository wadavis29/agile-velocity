According to the 16th annual State of Agile report, 44% of respondents reported that achieving predictability is how they prioritized the implementation of Agile ways of working. This was the #2 reason. Yet, it's common to hear that you can't have fixed delivery dates in this type of environment. What gives?

Enterprise Coaches Colleen Johnson and Randy Hale discuss and debate if Agile predictability is achievable, different methods of getting there (and why some of them don't work), and how important it is for organizations to align on what they need to be predictable.
